About Us

The creators of this app are students from IU creating a software that allows you to rent movie tickets. Their names are Dalton Graber, Jack Ventura, and Tyler Yocom. We started this project due to our local movie theater shutting down. We all have a special love for movies and want to see the industry thrive where we live. We hope to make our neighborhood into a theater hub.
